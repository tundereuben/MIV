MIV REAWAKENING STRATEGIES

Ministers’ and Leadership Conferences

MIV organizes interdenominational leadership conferences for Christian leaders in all spheres of influence including government executives, captains of industries, religious leaders, traditional rulers, and emerging leaders to equip them for kingdom-minded leadership. These conferences are held at both international and regional levels.

The annual International Ministers’ and Leadership Conference is held on the third week of January, every year at the international headquarters of The Men of Issachar Vision Inc behind First Bank, Olororo Junction, Ojoo, Ibadan with leaders from the local and international communities in attendance.

The Regional Ministers’ and Leadership Conferences are held in various regions in Nigeria and in other nations of the world as scheduled for each year.

To find a Ministers’ and Leadership Conference closest to your location, please check this year’ program calendar.

Family Life Conference

MIV champions the cause of raising healthy Christian homes by dedicating the month of May to nurturing families. Every last weekend in May, we organize Family Life Conference, a three-day conference which features practical teachings, question and answer segment, a three course dinner for couples of all ages. This interdenominational meeting has an attendance of over 500 couples annually. The conference is aimed at bringing healing season for families and to nourish and strengthen relationships. This conference is held at our international headquarters behind First Bank, Olororo Junction, Ojoo, Ibadan.

Back to Bethel Retreats (BBR)

BBR is a 3-day spiritual getaway for elect ladies and ministers wives. The purpose of this retreat is to align, nurse, nurture, shape and sharpen the vision and purpose of women in ministry. This retreat features sessions of the Word, intensive intercessions, health and lifestyle teachings, sports and exercise, inter-ministerial networking among others.

This retreat is held in various regions both in Nigeria and in other countries starting with the March edition held in Ibadan every year.

To find and register for a BBR Retreat closest to your location, please check this year’ program calendar.

Campus Repositioning (CaRe) & Corpers’ Repositioning (CoRe)

CaRe and CoRe are MIV’s strategic reawakening outreaches to the Christian communities in intuitions of higher learning and national youth service corps members. The goal is to ignite and keep aflame the fire of the undiluted truth of God’s word and sound doctrine amongst the next generation of believers and leaders. We build influence through partnership with their various organized bodies to train them in discipleship, missions and leadership and other need-based engagement.

To enlist your campus or corper’s fellowship for a CaRe or CoRe enagement, please contact our international head office at 144, Oyo road, behind first bank, Olororo bus-stop, Ojoo. U. I. P.O. Box 21066, Ibadan, Oyo state, Nigeria.

Alone With God Prayer Retreat (AWG)

AWG is a weekend interdenominational retreat for spiritual revival held in different locations across the world. This ‘stay-in’ retreat emphasizes the need for renewal of spiritual energy in the place of concentrated communion with God. The retreat features sessions of personal fellowship with God in worship, word and prayers as well as corporate sessions.

To find an AWG Retreat closest to your location, please check this year’ program calendar.

Let the Fire Fall Prayer Conference

MIV hosts this annual 3-day prayer conference on the last 3days of August to conclude her annual 1 month fasting and prayer which started since 1991. This spiritual activity was birthed and is sustained by a covenant with God following the resuscitation of 15 missionaries who died after a spiritual attack during a major missions outreach.

Join us at this years’ edition of Let the Fire Fall Prayer Conference from 29th to 31st of August at our international headquarters behind First Bank, Olororo Junction, Ojoo, Ibadan.

Leadership Update

Leadership Update is a quarterly mentorship leadership forum to strengthen the hands of individual leaders, counseled, ministered to and provoked to fulfill the ministry God has called them into and to build ministerial interrelationships.

Leadeship Update holds on the first Mondays in March, June, September, and December at our international headquarters behind First Bank, Olororo Junction, Ojoo, Ibadan. Time: 9am.

Awakening Publishing House and Media Production

The Awakening Publishing House and Media Production is the publishing and media arm of the ministry where printed materials including The Awakening Word Today-a daily devotional for spiritual reawakening and growth and life changing books like Not Offended, The Spirituality of Ministry, The Light Bearers, Running Well, Securing Your Spouse, Dressing for Your Destination among others authored by Rev Samson Ajetomobi and Rev Stella Ajetomobi are published.

This ministry also oversees the mass production and distribution of tapes, Cds and also provides and manages content for Awakening Voice and Awakening Word Telecast- the radio and TV broadcast of the Men of Issachar vision.

Join Rev Samson Ajetomobi on Dove TV every Thursday at 12pm for The Awakening Word Telecast.

For details on materials by Rev’d Samson and Stella Ajetomobi and airing please contact our international head office at 144, Oyo road, behind first bank, Olororo bus-stop, Ojoo. U. I. P.O. Box 21066, Ibadan, Oyo state, Nigeria.
